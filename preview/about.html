I'm a freelance graphic and motion designer from the North-East area of England.

I'm constantly striving to become better and better at what I do to make beautiful, yet functional art. From 3D to 2D, from advertising to telling stories, I'm here if you need to confide in me. If you're interested, please do reach out for queries, commissions or anything else.

When I'm not busy designing, I like to go out with friends, act like I'm a metal vocalist and game.

My design journey began back in 2017 when...

I took on an interest in design for the very first time. I was a 10-11 year old and wanted to try and make a GFX profile picture for a game I really liked. I found a paintdotnet tutorial and did exactly that. It... wasn't good and wasn't a particularly impressive tutorial either but I enjoyed it.

In 2018, I wanted to create a YouTube channel and tried making my own animated intro at the start. I used a free webapp called Panzoid before using After Effects later that year. This was also something I enjoyed doing and dedicated myself to it. I've broadened my horizons from there to advertising, branding work and more and I'm not going to stop.

Thank you for taking a look!
